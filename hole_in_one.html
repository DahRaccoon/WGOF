Challenge Summary

The WGOF Hole-in-One Challenge is simple: a yearly buy-in creates a large pot of cash, and the first member to sink an ace wins the entire pot!

  • The Buy-In: $20.00 per player each year.
  • The Prize: The entire pool goes to the first WGOF member to score a Hole-in-One during any sanctioned round played with a WGOF member.
  • Carryover: If the pot is not won, it rolls over to the next year, increasing the jackpot!
