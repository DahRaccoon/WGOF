Birdie Token Challenge

Greene Hills Country Club - All-Time Birdie Tracking

Challenge Summary

The WGOF Birdie Token Challenge is a season-long competition for bragging rights and a cash payout at the end of the year.

  • The Goal: Own the most tokens (one for each of the 18 holes at Green Hills) by the end of the year.
  • The Buy-In: $41.00 one-time fee.
  • Earning Tokens: Score a Birdie or Better on any hole while playing your own ball with a WGOF member witness. Report to the text group to claim the hole. This earns you the token, stealing it from the current owner if already owned.
  • The Payout: Winnings are based on final ownership:
    • $8.00 for every token you own.
    • The overall leader gets a $60.00 Bonus.
