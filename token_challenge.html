Challenge Summary

The WGOF Birdie Token Challenge is a season-long competition for bragging rights and a cash payout at the end of the year.

  • The Goal: Own the most tokens (one for each of the 18 holes at Green Hills) by the end of the year.
  • The Buy-In: $41.00 one-time fee.
  • Earning Tokens: Score a Birdie or Better on any hole while playing your own ball with a WGOF member witness and report the winning to the WGOF text group to claim the hole. This action earns you the token for that hole, stealing it from the current owner if aready owned.
  • The Payout: Winnings are based on your final ownership count:
    • You get $8.00 for every token you own.
    • The overall leader (most tokens owned) gets a $60.00 Bonus.
